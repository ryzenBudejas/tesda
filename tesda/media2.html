Carousel


Our Carousel is a robust and versatile component that can be an image slider, to an item carousel, to
an onboarding experience. It is touch enabled making it especially smooth to use on mobile.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.


Full Width Slider

Our carousel has a full width option that makes it into a simple and elegant image carousel. You can
also have indicators that show up on the bottom of the slider.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.

Special Options

The carousel doesn't only support images but also allows you to make content carousels. You can add
fixed items to your carousel by adding a div with the class carousel-fixed-item and adding your
fixed content in there.

Note: This is also touch compatible! Try swiping with your finger to scroll through the carousel.
